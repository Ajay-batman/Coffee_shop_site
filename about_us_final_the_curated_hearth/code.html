Our Story

"It started with a single cast-iron hearth and a bag of sun-dried beans from the highlands of Ethiopia."

Founded by Elias and Sarah Thorne, The Curated Hearth was born from a desire to return to the slow, intentional methods of brewing. We didn't want a shop that felt like a factory; we wanted a living room for the neighborhood.

The name reflects our philosophy: the hearth is the center of the home, a place of warmth and nourishment. We've spent over a decade perfecting the alchemy of the roast, ensuring that the legacy of the farmers is honored in every sip.
