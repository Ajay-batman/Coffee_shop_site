Getting to the curated hearth

directions_walk

By Foot

We are a 5-minute stroll from the Central Plaza. Follow the aroma of fresh cedar and roasting beans past the library.

directions_bus

Public Transit

Take the Green Line to 'Heritage Station'. Our doors are located just two blocks South-East of the main exit.

directions_car

Driving & Parking

Street parking is available on Espresso Lane. For longer stays, use the 'Bean City Garage' on 2nd Ave—we validate for 2 hours.
